Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, processes, certifications, and how to get started with Tvaraa Organics.

General Questions

What is Tvaraa Organics?

Tvaraa Organics is a leading organic contract manufacturing company specializing in the development and production of premium organic products. We offer comprehensive services including formulation development, manufacturing, packaging, and regulatory support for beauty, wellness, and personal care brands worldwide.

How long has Tvaraa Organics been in business?

We have been in business for over 15 years since our founding in 2009. During this time, we have built a reputation for excellence and have partnered with over 100 brands across 50+ countries.

What makes Tvaraa Organics different from other manufacturers?

Our commitment to 100% organic ingredients, state-of-the-art GMP-certified facilities, comprehensive regulatory support, and end-to-end service offerings set us apart. We combine traditional knowledge with modern technology to deliver exceptional quality products.

Services

What services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive manufacturing solutions including:

  • Contract Manufacturing
  • Private Label Services
  • Third Party Manufacturing
  • R&D and Formulation Development
  • Packaging Solutions
  • Regulatory and Compliance Support

Can you help with product formulation?

Absolutely! Our experienced R&D team can develop custom formulations based on your specifications, target market, and brand positioning. We work with you from initial concept through final product optimization.

Do you offer private label services?

Yes, we offer complete private label solutions including existing formulations, custom packaging design, branding support, and market-ready product development to help you launch your brand quickly.

Manufacturing Process

What is your manufacturing capacity?

Our 50,000+ square foot GMP-certified facility can handle orders ranging from small batches of 500 units to large-scale production runs of over 100,000 units. We have the flexibility to scale production based on your needs.

What types of products can you manufacture?

We manufacture a wide range of organic products including:

  • Skincare products (creams, serums, cleansers)
  • Hair care products (shampoos, conditioners, treatments)
  • Personal care items (body lotions, soaps, deodorants)
  • Baby care products
  • Health supplements (capsules, tablets, liquids)
  • Aromatherapy products and essential oils

How long does the manufacturing process take?

Timeline varies based on product complexity and order size. Typically, existing formulations take 2-4 weeks, while custom formulations require 6-12 weeks including development and testing. We provide detailed timelines during the consultation phase.

Quality & Certifications

What certifications do you have?

We maintain multiple international certifications including:

  • GMP (Good Manufacturing Practices)
  • ISO 9001:2015
  • USDA Organic
  • Halal Certification
  • Kosher Certification
  • FDA Registration
  • ECOCERT for Organic Cosmetics

How do you ensure product quality?

We implement rigorous quality control measures including raw material testing, in-process monitoring, finished product analysis, stability testing, and comprehensive documentation. Every batch is tested before release.

Are all your ingredients organic?

Yes, we are committed to using 100% organic and natural ingredients in our formulations. All ingredients are sourced from certified organic suppliers and undergo thorough quality verification.

Pricing & Orders

What are your minimum order quantities?

Minimum order quantities vary by product type and complexity. Generally, we start from 500 units for private label products and 1,000 units for custom formulations. Contact us for specific MOQ requirements for your project.

How is pricing determined?

Pricing is based on factors including formulation complexity, ingredients used, order quantity, packaging requirements, and additional services. We provide detailed, transparent quotes with no hidden fees.

Do you offer samples?

Yes, we provide samples for evaluation. For existing formulations, samples may be available after signing a confidentiality agreement. Custom formulation samples are provided during the development process.

Shipping & Logistics

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. We have established logistics partnerships to ensure safe and timely delivery of products globally, with full documentation and regulatory compliance support.

How are products packaged for shipping?

Products are carefully packaged using appropriate protective materials to prevent damage during transit. We use temperature-controlled shipping for sensitive products and provide tracking information for all shipments.

What documentation do you provide for international shipments?

We provide comprehensive documentation including certificates of analysis, ingredient lists, regulatory compliance documents, export certificates, and any other documentation required for customs clearance in your destination country.
